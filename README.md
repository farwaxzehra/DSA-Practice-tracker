A simple yet powerful **DSA (Data Structures & Algorithms) Practice Tracker** built with **Streamlit** to help you track your problem-solving progress, identify weak areas, and monitor performance over time.

---

## **Features**

- **Add Practice Data:** Log problems with **name, difficulty, topic, time taken, status (SOLVED/FAILED),** and **revision count**.  
- **Automatic Revision Tracking:** If a problem already exists, its **revision count** is incremented automatically.  
- **View All Data:** See all your practice entries in a clean, **tabular format**.  
- **Filter by Difficulty:** Focus on **Easy, Medium, or Hard** problems.  
- **Weak Topics Analysis:** Identify topics where you struggle by counting **failed attempts**.  
- **Average Time Tracker:** See the **average time** spent per problem.  
- **Problem Difficulty Metrics:** Quick summary of problems solved by **Easy, Medium, Hard** levels.
